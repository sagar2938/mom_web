ABOUT MOM

In today’s life most of the people stay away from their native place and miss mom food. So, using this mobile app MOM- Mother On Mission, one can order fresh “mom cooked healthy food” anytime and stay healthy.

The main objective of the founders of this app is to provide all healthy mom cooked food from all the regions of India and to keep alive some recipes which are getting extinct with women of every home.

Our company “Mother On Mission” (MOM) will collect the home cooked hygienic food from various MOMs (non working women) and will deliver it to all who miss mom cooked food in this busy society. We are working to provide a platform for women and is basically for their empowerment. All the recipes of the MOMs will be delivered in the market with their own names and identities which will makes them the “ MOMBOSS”. You will just have to cook some more what you cook the best and its our responsibility to make it a brand. So, please come and join MOM so that your food will not be food only it will be a brand.

Want tasty MOM cooked food delivered at your doorstep? Install MOM app now and order food from different MOM Chefs in your locality.
